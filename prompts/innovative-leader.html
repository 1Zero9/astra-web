The CRIT Framework

Your Foundation for Strategic Prompting

The most effective prompts follow a proven structure. Master CRIT, and you'll transform AI from a simple tool into your Strategic Thought Partner.

C

Context

Give rich background - what's the situation, constraints, stakeholders? The more strategic context you provide, the more strategic the output.

R

Role

Assign AI a specific expert identity - "Act as my strategic advisor." You can simulate any expert at your fingertips.

I

Interview

Let AI ask YOU clarifying questions - "Ask me 1-3 questions to gain deeper context." This is the secret weapon.

T

Task

Define the specific output you need - decision framework, strategic analysis, action plan.

Why CRIT Works

It transforms AI from a response generator into a Strategic Thought Partner that challenges your thinking, surfaces blind spots, and helps you escape operational overwhelm to focus on strategy.
